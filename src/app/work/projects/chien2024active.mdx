We develop joint active and passive beamforming strategies for RIS-assisted MIMO systems to minimize Symbol Error Rate (SER).  
The proposed approach leverages improved differential evolution for optimization.  

**Keywords:** RIS, MIMO, beamforming, SER minimization.
